05 — Interview Prep (Drip Mode)
How Drip Interviews Work
One question per day. Real feedback. Real growth.
1. Q serves one interview question per day
2. User writes their answer (free text)
3. AI evaluates: structure, depth, keywords, real-world grounding
4. Feedback highlights what to add next time
5. Full simulation mode: 5 questions, timed, full report
🟢 Junior QA
40 questions
"What's the difference between severity and priority?"
"Walk me through how you'd write a test case."
🔵 Mid-Level QA
35 questions
"How do you decide when to stop testing?"
"Explain your approach to regression testing in Agile."
🟡 Senior / Lead QA
30 questions
"How would you build a QA process from scratch?"
"How do you influence devs to prioritize quality?"
